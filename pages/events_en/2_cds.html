The dome version of Vaulted Harmonies was first presented in July 2026 at the planetarium of the Cité des Sciences et de l’Industrie as the evening event of the 2026 AES International Conference on Audio for Virtual and Augmented Reality and Immersive Games.

The dome master version was derived from the 360° release of the movie using Blender and the FFmpeg toolkit. Following an initial series of tests conducted both in the planetarium and in VR simulations, the virtual horizon was tilted upward by 40°. This adaptation repositioned the performers and key architectural elements toward the central viewing region of the dome, improving visual comfort and scene readability.

The third-order ambisonic soundtrack was decoded to the planetarium's 5.1 + zenith loudspeaker configuration using the IEM Ambisonics Decoder.
