Vaulted Harmonies was presented on the Coraulis platform in May 2026 during the Urbaphone workshop in Nantes. Coraulis combines a 360° projection screen with a loudspeaker array supporting third-order ambisonic reproduction. Originally developed for students and researchers at the ENSA Nantes School of Architecture, the platform provides an immersive environment for the presentation and exploration of architectural and urban design projects.

Audience members experiencing Vaulted Harmonies on the Coraulis platform during the Urbaphone workshop.

To adapt Vaulted Harmonies to Coraulis, the equirectangular movie was projected onto a digital model of the platform's flared cylindrical screen in Blender. The third-order ambisonic soundtrack was decoded in real time to the loudspeaker array using the SPARTA Ambisonic Decoder.
