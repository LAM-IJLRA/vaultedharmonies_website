CNRS-MITI Workshop (Paris, April 2026)

Vaulted Harmonies was presented during a workshop organised by the CNRS Mission for Interdisciplinary and Cross-Cutting Initiatives (MITI) at the Marie Curie Auditorium in Paris. The screening brought together researchers from a wide range of disciplines, including the humanities, musicology, and history. The event provided an opportunity to showcase the potential of acoustic simulation and archaeoacoustic reconstruction methods, while fostering new interdisciplinary collaborations within the CNRS community.

Meeting of the Acoustical Society of Japan (Tokyo, March 2026)

Vaulted Harmonies was presented during a meeting of the Acoustical Society of Japan in Tokyo. The concert was reproduced over a 7.1.4 loudspeaker system, allowing attendees to experience the spatial reconstruction of Notre-Dame's historical soundscapes.

IoA Auditorium Acoustics Conference (Bristol, September 2025)

The project was featured at the IoA Auditorium Acoustics Conference, organised by the Institute of Acoustics. The concert was presented on a 20.4 loudspeaker array as part of the conference programme dedicated to auditorium design and acoustic perception.

European Heritage Days (Paris and Limonest, September 2025)

As part of the European Heritage Days, Vaulted Harmonies was presented both on the Sorbonne University campus and at the Agora Auditorium in Limonest. These public screenings introduced audiences to the project's reconstruction of the evolving architecture, acoustics, and musical practices of Notre-Dame Cathedral.

Forum Acusticum / Euronoise (Málaga, June 2025)

Vaulted Harmonies was presented on June 24th at the Trade Fairs and Congress Center of Málaga during Forum Acusticum / Euronoise 2025. The event provided an opportunity to share the project with the international acoustics community.

French Congress on Acoustics (Paris, April 2025)

The concert was presented on April 29th at Sorbonne University's International Conference Center during the 17th French Congress on Acoustics, bringing together researchers, engineers, and practitioners from across the field of acoustics.

AES and SFA Audio3D'24 Conference (Paris, November 2024)

One of the first public presentations of Vaulted Harmonies took place during the Audio3D'24 conference, jointly organised by the Audio Engineering Society and the French Acoustical Society. The concert was projected at the Conservatoire National Supérieur de Musique et de Danse de Paris (CNSMDP).
